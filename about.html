Our Story

Founded in 2010, Aroma Coffee began with a simple mission: to bring exceptional coffee experiences to every cup.

🌱

A Journey Through Coffee

What started as a small roastery in Seattle has grown into a global coffee community. We travel to the world's most renowned coffee-growing regions, building relationships with farmers and learning the art of cultivation.

Every bean we select tells a story of dedication, from the careful tending of coffee plants to the precise roasting process that brings out each origin's unique characteristics.

We believe coffee is more than just a beverage—it's a connection between cultures, a moment of pause in a busy day, and an art form worth perfecting.
